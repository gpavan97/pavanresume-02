College-Project-(Secure Auditing & De-Duplicating Data In Cloud)

Description:

  • As the cloud computing technology develops during the last decade, which benefits in sparing efforts on heavy data maintenance and management

  • Nevertheless,the outsourced cloud storage is not fully trustworthy.

  • we study the problem of integrity auditing and secure de-duplication on cloud data.

  • Specifically, aiming at achieving both data integrity and deduplication in cloud.

  • we propose two secure systems, namely SecCloud and SecCloud+.

  • Our proposed SecCloud system has achieved both integrity auditing and file deduplication.

  • SecCloud introduces an auditing entity with a maintenance of a MapReduce cloud, uploading as well as audit the integrity of data having been stored in cloud.
